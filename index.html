What is Anti-Monopoly?

Anti-Monopoly is similar to Monopoly, but with one major division: at the start, all players are evenly divided into "Competitors" and "Monopolists." Competitors can build on any street they own, and build 4 houses before building an apartment, receive the same rent for transport companies no matter how many they own, and generally receive less money at a time, but lose less at a time.
